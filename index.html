The real blocker to digital transformation: nobody starts from the actual way work is done.

Too many projects start “solution first”: new platform, new AI tool, new automation — without validating what the business actually does today. Each handoff between management, IT, and operations distorts the intent. After a few months, nobody can say with confidence “this is our process.” That’s the blind spot Mimix removes.
