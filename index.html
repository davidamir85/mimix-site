How Mimix works

AI-guided discovery that always produces a consistent outcome.

1. AI interviews the doer

Guided conversation, real-time follow-ups, extraction of inputs, outputs, roles, KPIs, systems, and exceptions — the things a good business analyst would ask, but faster.

2. Compare and align

Interview multiple people for the same process. Mimix highlights gaps and contradictions and builds a single version of the truth.

3. Pull in other signals

Training materials, dashboards, customer feedback, even marketing collateral — Mimix can use these as context to complete the process view.

4. See the real as-is

Get a structured description of how the process actually runs today — actors, systems, steps, variants, and pain points. Now you can measure, not speculate.

5. Plan the to-be

With the as-is agreed, you can design the to-be that matches your business outcome — whether that’s AI assistance, RPA, system change, or training.
